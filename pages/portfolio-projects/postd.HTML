Celebrating Community

Where It All Began

The Community & Connection Pocket Tarot started as a part of the AIGA DC SHINE Mentorship Program. I was a mentee eager to combine my love of print design and tarot. To build a sense of community between other artists and designers with D.C. natives, community leaders, and D.C. transplants all while highlighting the lesser-known community spaces or pieces of history that aren’t talked about enough right in Washington, D.C.

This project was born out of a desire to educate others about tarot and to honor the exceptional craftsmanship of our artists and illustrators. Their contributions are the heart and soul of this project, and we can’t thank them enough for their dedication and talent.

Project Scope

Artwork Selection

By working with local, volunteer artists to curate the artwork for the deck, it reflects Washington, D.C.’s diversity through the varying art styles of each individual artist. Furthermore, this deck highlights artwork created by Romani artists, some of whom aren’t from D.C., but have cultural ties with tarot.

Layout Design

Each tarot card will be designed in a way that honors the traditional symbolism associated with minor arcana suits; while honoring the cultural symbolism and landmarks throughout Washington, D.C. Also an accompanying guidebook featuring a forward by author Jezmina Von Theila was created to help readers deepen their connection to the deck.

Print & Production

Working with local printers to produce high-quality, pocket-sized tarot decks, while ensuring the printing process adheres to environmentally sustainable practices wherever possible.

Packaging & Distribution

Coordinating with local printers and small businesses throughout the Washington,D.C. area to stock and sell the tarot decks and guidebooks.

Creating Something Magical

As a part of my commitment to cultivating community, the Community & Connection Pocket Tarot is entirely funded through local small business sponsors, community events, grants and donations. Visit our website for updates on when pre-orders will be available, meet the contributing artists, and get tickets to our events.

Cultivating Community

The Community & Connection Pocket Tarot Deck celebrates Washington, D.C.’s diversity; while fostering a sense of pride and belonging amongst it’s residents.

Cultural Representation

Featuring local artists, the BiPoC, and LGBTQ+ communities, this deck enhances the representation and recognition of marginalized communities and how they shaped our nation’s capital.

Educational Resource

The Community & Connection Tarot Guidebook serves as an educational resource highlighting the history of tarot and it’s deep rooted connection to Romani culture. Along with insights and references to Washington, D.C.’s cultural heritage and history.
